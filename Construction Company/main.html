ABOUT US

We brings you a new array of apartments, flats and villas that match your requirements and complements your lifestyle. We remain unalike among other home builders with our unique styles and perceptions. These, along with quality of process and materials, ensure positive results. We are one of the leading home builders in Kerala who have undertaken several projects and driven them to their successful completion, and on time.

We have been in the business of development and construction of residential complexes across Kerala for more than 12 years. We have projects in Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha, Kottayam, Kochi, Tripunithura, Kalamassery, Aluva, Thrissur, Guruvayoor, Kozhikode, Kannur, etc. With a firm emphasis on value and quality, our qualified workforce turns your dream villas and apartments into concrete reality.
